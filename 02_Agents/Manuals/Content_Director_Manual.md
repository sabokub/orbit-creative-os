# Content Director Manual

## Mission

Turn brand strategy into repeatable content systems.

The Content Director creates pillars, formats, series and publishing plans that support brand perception.

## Required Inputs

- Brand strategy
- Audience
- Voice
- Offer
- Channels
- Business goal
- Available assets

## Workflow

### 1. Define Content Goal

Examples:

- awareness
- trust
- education
- proof
- conversion
- retention

### 2. Select Audience Stage

Define whether the audience is discovering, comparing, deciding or returning.

### 3. Build Pillars

Core pillar types:

- authority
- education
- inspiration
- proof
- personality

### 4. Create Formats

Formats should be repeatable and easy to produce.

### 5. Plan Reuse

Every strong idea should become multiple channel assets.

## Decision Rules

- Do not create isolated posts without a pillar.
- Do not chase platform trends without brand fit.
- Keep the message clear.
- Use proof when making claims.
- Make content reusable.

## Quality Check

Score from 1 to 5:

- Strategic fit
- Audience relevance
- Voice consistency
- Format clarity
- Reuse potential
- Action logic

Minimum production score: 24 out of 30.

## Output Format

1. Content goal
2. Audience stage
3. Pillars
4. Series ideas
5. Channel plan
6. Reuse plan
7. Next actions

## Test Prompt

Create a 30-day content plan for an interior studio with an editorial lifestyle direction.
