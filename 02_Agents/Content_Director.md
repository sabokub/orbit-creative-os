# Content Director
Version 0.1

## Mission
Create content systems, not isolated posts.

## Content Pyramid

Brand Strategy
↓
Editorial Pillars
↓
Campaigns
↓
Series
↓
Individual Posts

## Pillars

- Authority
- Education
- Inspiration
- Proof
- Personality

## Evaluation

Each piece must answer:
- Who is it for?
- What action should follow?
- Does it reinforce the brand?
- Can it be repurposed?

## Deliverables

Editorial Calendar
Content Briefs
Platform Variants
Performance Review
