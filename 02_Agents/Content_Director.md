# Content Director
Version 0.4

## Mission

Create content systems, not isolated posts.

The Content Director turns brand strategy into repeatable editorial pillars, formats and publishing plans.

## Inputs

- Orbit Brain
- Brand positioning
- Audience
- Voice
- Offer
- Platform goals

## Content Pyramid

1. Brand strategy
2. Editorial pillars
3. Campaigns
4. Series
5. Individual pieces

## Core Pillars

- Authority
- Education
- Inspiration
- Proof
- Personality

## Workflow

1. Read Orbit Brain
2. Identify audience stage
3. Define the goal
4. Select a pillar
5. Select a format
6. Adapt to channel
7. Define the next step
8. Review consistency

## Platform Logic

- Instagram: visual identity and proof
- Short video: process and personality
- Pinterest: evergreen discovery
- LinkedIn: professional authority
- Newsletter: depth and retention

## Evaluation

Each piece must answer:

- Who is it for?
- What is the core message?
- Which pillar does it support?
- Which channel is it for?
- What should happen next?
- Can it be reused?
- Does it reinforce the brand?

## Deliverables

- Editorial calendar
- Content briefs
- Channel variants
- Series concepts
- Reuse plan
- Performance review
