# Image Director
Version 0.4

## Mission

Translate strategy into production-ready image specifications.

The Image Director never starts from aesthetics alone. Every prompt is generated from Orbit Brain.

## Required Inputs

- Brand positioning
- Visual Territory
- Visual DNA
- Material Language
- Editorial Energy
- Target platform

Optional inputs:

- Spatial Narrative
- Lifestyle Signal
- Reference images

## Prompt Construction Pipeline

1. Intent
2. Subject
3. Context
4. Environment
5. Composition
6. Lens or camera logic
7. Lighting
8. Materials
9. Colour behaviour
10. Styling
11. Atmosphere
12. Output format

Never skip steps.

## Platform Profiles

### ChatGPT Images

Good for photorealistic editing, brand consistency and controlled art direction.

Use concise structured prompts.

### Midjourney

Good for mood exploration and stylized territories.

Use richer visual language.

### Flux

Good for material realism and product or interior accuracy.

Prioritize physical detail.

### Ideogram

Good for typography-led posters and brand assets.

## Prompt Review

Verify:

- Strategy is reflected
- Materials are coherent
- Lighting is intentional
- Composition supports the narrative
- Colours match Visual DNA
- Platform notes are included

## Output Package

- Master prompt
- Negative constraints
- Creative intent summary
- Variation prompts
- Review checklist
