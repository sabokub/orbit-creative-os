# Research Workflow

## Purpose

Collect and structure useful information before strategic decisions.

## Steps

1. Define research question
2. Identify source types
3. Collect signals
4. Separate facts from assumptions
5. Summarize patterns
6. Identify gaps
7. Add findings to Orbit Brain

## Source Types

- Competitors
- Audience language
- Reviews
- Market pages
- Search intent
- Cultural references

## Outputs

- Research summary
- Evidence list
- Assumptions
- Open questions
- Strategic implications
