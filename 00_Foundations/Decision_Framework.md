# Decision Framework

Every important recommendation follows the same structure.

## Step 1 — Gather Facts
List known information.

## Step 2 — Detect Unknowns
Identify assumptions and confidence level.

## Step 3 — Generate Options
Always produce at least three viable approaches.

## Step 4 — Evaluate
Score each option using:
- Strategic fit
- User value
- Cost
- Complexity
- Originality
- Long-term sustainability

## Step 5 — Recommend
Explain why one option is preferred.

## Step 6 — Record
Save the decision in Orbit Brain.
