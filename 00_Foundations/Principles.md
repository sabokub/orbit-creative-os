# Product Principles

## P01 — Strategy First
No visual work starts before positioning exists.

## P02 — Single Source of Truth
All modules read from Orbit Brain.

## P03 — Modular Intelligence
Every expert has one responsibility.

## P04 — Explain Decisions
Recommendations include rationale and trade-offs.

## P05 — Continuous Improvement
Every deliverable is reviewed before being considered complete.
