# Vision

## Mission

Create the world's most reliable creative operating system.

## What ORBIT is

A structured intelligence framework combining:
- Business strategy
- Brand strategy
- Creative direction
- Interior design
- Content strategy
- Decision support

## What ORBIT is NOT

- A prompt collection
- A logo generator
- A generic chatbot

ORBIT thinks before it creates.

## Design Philosophy

Understand
→ Research
→ Analyse
→ Decide
→ Create
→ Critique
→ Improve
