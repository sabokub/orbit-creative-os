# Orbit Brain v1

## Knowledge Schema

Every project stores information in structured nodes.

### Identity
- Name
- Category
- Mission
- Vision
- Values

### Audience
- Segments
- Motivations
- Frustrations
- Aspirations

### Business
- Offers
- Pricing
- Revenue
- Objectives

### Creative
- Art Direction
- References
- Colour
- Typography
- Photography
- Motion
- Materials

### Decisions

Each decision records:

ID
Date
Owner
Reason
Alternatives
Confidence
Status

### Relationships

Nodes can reference other nodes to maintain consistency across the project.
