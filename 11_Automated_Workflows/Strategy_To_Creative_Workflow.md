# Workflow — Strategy to Creative Direction

## Goal

Convert strategy into creative rules.

## Steps

1. Orbit Core confirms strategic clarity
2. Orbit Creative defines visual territory
3. Orbit Creative defines visual DNA
4. Orbit Creative translates references into rules
5. Orbit Critic checks coherence
6. Approved creative direction is saved

## Outputs

- Visual territory
- Visual DNA
- Art direction rules
- Creative risks
