# Workflow — Content Production

## Goal

Turn strategy into a repeatable content system.

## Steps

1. Orbit Content reads the strategy
2. Content pillars are defined
3. Monthly themes are set
4. Formats are assigned by channel
5. Reuse logic is defined
6. Orbit Critic reviews weak ideas
7. Final content plan is approved

## Outputs

- Content pillars
- Monthly plan
- Channel plan
- Reuse plan
