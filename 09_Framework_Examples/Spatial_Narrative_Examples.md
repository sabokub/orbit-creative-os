# Spatial Narrative Examples

## Example 1 — Small Apartment

Arrival: immediate clarity and breathing space

Discovery: layered storage and visible personality

Immersion: seating zone that also works on camera

Memory: one mirror or corner that becomes the signature image

## Example 2 — Coffee Shop

Arrival: smell, menu focus and first visual signature

Discovery: service counter and pastry display

Immersion: comfortable seating and texture

Memory: branded corner, light moment or hero material detail
