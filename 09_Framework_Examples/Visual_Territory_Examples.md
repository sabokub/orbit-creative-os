# Visual Territory Examples

## Example 1 — Editorial Interior Studio

Primary emotion: cool confidence

Secondary emotion: lived-in sophistication

Colour behaviour: neutral base with controlled blue and green accents

Lighting language: flash, directional daylight and warm evening practicals

Composition language: close crops, slightly wide lens, layered foregrounds

Styling language: books, drinks, texture, magazines and human traces

## Example 2 — Boutique Coffee Shop

Primary emotion: ritual pleasure

Secondary emotion: community warmth

Colour behaviour: espresso brown, cream, oxidized metal and muted red accent

Lighting language: low warm pools with stronger daylight contrast

Composition language: table-level views, material close-ups and service moments
