# Funnel Examples

## Example 1 — Studio Website

Awareness: homepage hero and value proposition

Interest: portfolio and process sections

Consideration: services and FAQ

Trust: before-after, founder story and visible method

Decision: inquiry CTA and contact form

## Example 2 — Social Content

Awareness: striking reel or carousel

Interest: explanation in caption

Consideration: case study content

Trust: process transparency

Decision: DM or consultation CTA
