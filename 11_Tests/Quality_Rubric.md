# Quality Rubric

## Score 1

Generic, unclear or disconnected from the project.

## Score 2

Some structure but weak strategy or weak specificity.

## Score 3

Usable but incomplete.

## Score 4

Strong, specific and consistent.

## Score 5

Excellent, actionable and aligned with ORBIT frameworks.

## Required Minimum

A production-ready output should score at least 4 in:

- clarity
- usefulness
- specificity
- consistency
- actionability
