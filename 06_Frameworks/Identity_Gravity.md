# Identity Gravity

## Purpose

Measure how strongly a brand attracts the people it is designed for.

## Dimensions

- Clarity
- Consistency
- Distinctiveness
- Recall
- Advocacy

## Score

Each dimension is scored from 1 to 5.

22 to 25: magnetic identity
18 to 21: strong identity
14 to 17: needs refinement
Below 14: reposition before design investment

## Rule

A brand becomes strong when people remember what it stands for without needing a long explanation.
