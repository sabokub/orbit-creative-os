# Material Language™

Evaluate every material through:

- Texture
- Temperature
- Reflection
- Durability
- Authenticity
- Maintenance
- Cost
- Emotional impact

Limit palettes to a coherent family rather than maximizing variety.

# Material Roles

Every palette should contain:

- Hero material
- Structural material
- Accent material
- Softening material

Avoid using more than one hero material in the same visual composition.
