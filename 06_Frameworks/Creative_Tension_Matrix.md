# Creative Tension Matrix

## Purpose

Define a brand by balancing intentional opposites.

## Axes

- Premium to accessible
- Timeless to contemporary
- Minimal to expressive
- Confident to playful
- Exclusive to inclusive
- Calm to energetic

## Method

Do not aim for the middle.

Choose a precise position on each axis and explain why it supports the strategy.

## Output

- Selected axis positions
- Rationale
- Visual impact
- Messaging impact
- Risks
