# Spatial Narrative™

## Purpose
Design spaces that tell a coherent story instead of assembling attractive objects.

## Narrative Layers

1. Identity
Who lives, works or visits here?

2. Intention
What should people feel within 10 seconds?

3. Journey
Arrival → Discovery → Immersion → Memory

4. Signature Moments
Define 1–3 memorable scenes worth photographing.

## Validation

A visitor should be able to describe the personality of the space without seeing the brand name.
